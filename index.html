Welcome to our new team Thank You and Aknowledments website. A place where we will update the Nlakapamux Nation on how the Tl'kemchEEn Warriors Youth Basketball team has been doing in their training, fundraising and publicly thank the sponsors and donations that are received,

The Team

Tlâ€™kemchEEn Warriors Youth Basketball is a parent led basketball program running out of the Lytton Area. This is the 4th year where youth aged 8-17 years from the Lytton area practice and develop their basketball skills to train and represent the Nlakapamux Nation at annual Jr All Native Basketball Tournament (JANT).

The team is made up into 4 divisions (Under 13 Girls, Under 13 Boys, Under 17 Girls & Under 17 Boys. This past year 51 youth were in the 2 Lytton gyms to attend practice twice a week. Since September 2019 they have achieved over 100 physical activity hours.
